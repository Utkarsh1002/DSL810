About the Course

This course is being taught by Prof. Jay Dhariwal. This course would provide a hands-on introduction to internet of things prototyping to aid in thedesign and fabrication of smart systems. The students should be able to build IoT based products or experimental setups for their research through the skills learnt in this class.
